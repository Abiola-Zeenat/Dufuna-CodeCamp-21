Company Profile

With the increase use and changing of technology every day. It seems like advanced computer knowledge and skills will be a basic skill for almost every day transactions and operation. As such, there is a need for individuals with the zeal to acquire these skills from professionals. SocioTech Solutions is a computer technology company that offers advanced skills in computing and application.

SocioTech Solutions is a Malawian Tech-Biz consultancy Company. It derives its inspiration from striving to merge information technology and Social Sciences. As such, we provide expert services and products in IT and Social Sciences arena. These services can be accessed both remotely through our Store or at our physical offices in Mzuzu City, Luwinga Road, Opposite Petroda Filling Station.
